Graphic Design

With over six years professional design experience, stunning visuals are second nature to every project I am a part of. Take a look at some of my recent career highlights.

Annual Awards 2024

In my work with the British Dyslexia Association, I had the opportunity to pitch my ideas to the marketing and wider working teams. Presented with 3 branding visions, the marketing team and I decided on this concept, keeping in line with the class and glitz of the 5 star venue which would host the Awards for 2024. From my initial artwork concept, I expanded this to cover all printed materials for the evening, as well as social media marketing. The branding added style to the evening from the programme brochure, stage backdrops and even details like table numbers, which was all a hit with guests, the like of which included Theo Paphitis and Lizzie Acker.

Dyslexia Awareness Week

Another pitch which was successful, is the artwork concept for #DAW24. Going from a loose concept around storytelling, I solidified the campaign through my visual representation. The branding all came together after my initial design of the campaign logo, representing the unique pages of each person's life and the opportunity to always change the direction of your story, the aim of raising awareness around dyslexia.

DAW is all about social media presence. From my direction, social media assets for all platforms were created to share case stories, statistics, and campaign messages, to name a few. Due to the visual strength of this project, the charity gained significantly higher engagement, building support around dyslexia and dyscalculia.
